How I approached the Respiratory SCE

// under medicine resp exams

Introduction

A plan of attack is necessary. Here is mine.

Timing

I began approximately 6 months before the exam.

Books

  • Revision Notes for the Respiratory Medicine Specialty Certificate Examination by Caroline Patterson and Meg Coleman. I found this immensely helpful in clarifying what the entry level of knowledge required was.

  • Oxford handbook of respiratory medicine. I read it cover to cover. It felt helpful.

Guidelines

  • It is necessary to read and know well all of the bts guidelines. I did this.

On line question banks

  • I did the RCP Respiratory SCE question bank, the questions on the BTS website, and the questions provided with the BTS Respiratory SCE course. Helpful.

Courses

  • I went on the BTS Respiratory SCE course.

Result

Passed first time with an average score. In retrospect I wish I had given more little more time to knowing the guidelines really well because so many questions were drawn from these.
